    PRC Presantation(Canva)

    The Digital Divide refers to the gap difference between individuals or communities that have access to information and communication technologies and those that do not or have restricted access. This gap encloses an imbalance in access to the internet, computers, smartphones, and other digital technologies, leading to inequalities in opportunities and resources. In this project we had to discuss the digital divide in South Africa

    CNF Scenario Assignment(Word)

    Five scenarios pertaining to the design and security of wireless networks are included in this assignment. Aspects like frequency band selection, access point placement, security measures (like WPA3 encryption), site surveys, network design, scalability, redundancy, and potential threats like man-in-the-middle attacks or unauthorized access are all covered in each scenario, which covers a particular context like office buildings, urban settings, or retail stores. The assignment offers comprehensive network planning techniques, including everything from regulatory compliance to bandwidth management.
